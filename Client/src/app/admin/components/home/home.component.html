About Us

Established 1997
Dry Clean City is a well-established Dry Cleaner, situated in the northern suburbs of Johannesburg. We specialise in Dry Cleaning, Laundry Services, Alterations and Shoe Repairs. Our dedicated management team and well-trained staff work hard and effortlessly to give our customers a great experience. We currently have three brick and mortar stores where we cater for our domestic clients. Our main store is located at Riverside shopping centre in Bryanston where we do all our dry cleaning and cater for our corporate and contract clients. We strive to deliver exceptional service, to ensure we sustain and grow our business. We continuously invest in training programs to ensure we can entrust ourselves to take good care of our customers.
